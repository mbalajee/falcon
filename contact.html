Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request a quote for your products?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form above, calling our sales team directly, or by emailing info@falconelectrooptics.com with details about your requirements.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our products internationally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact our sales team for specific information.

What is your warranty policy?

Most of our products come with a standard one-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available for select products. Please refer to the product documentation or contact our support team for details.

Can you provide custom solutions for specific requirements?

Absolutely! We specialize in developing custom electro-optical solutions tailored to our clients' specific needs. Please contact our engineering team to discuss your requirements.
